# Resend Design System

## Overview
Resend's design system focuses on email communication with clean, modern interfaces for transactional and marketing emails.

## Components
- Email Templates
- Transactional Emails
- Marketing Campaigns
- Notification Systems
- Analytics Dashboards

## Design Tokens
- Colors: Brand colors with accessibility
- Typography: Web-safe fonts
- Spacing: Email-safe spacing
- Images: Optimized for email clients

## Usage Guidelines
- Design for email client compatibility
- Include alt text for images
- Use table-based layouts when necessary
- Test across email clients
